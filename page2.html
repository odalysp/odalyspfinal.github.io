Change on an Individual Level

Although it may not always feel like a large difference, small changes in our daily lives can help reduce our personal carbon footprint.

Reusable Bottles

Although you may have heard this many times, reusable bottles are defintely a way to help reduce your carbon footprint. It can be easy to forget but reusing bottles helps reduce the energy needed that it takes to break down other bottles.

Purchasing Choices

Be more mindful of the products that you are using. Buy products that come with less packaging and with packaging that can be recycled. Check labels for this information!

Manage your Individual Waste More Closely

When purchasing prodcuts be mindful of the stock you have.

Purchasing Choices - Larger Scale

If you are looking to buy a new car you can look into cars that are better for the environment. There are effeicnt hybrid and smart cars that can help reduce your carbon footprint.

Purchasing Choices - Larger Scale

If you own a home or know someone who does, reccomend looking at technologies that are better for the environment. For example, solar panels can be installed in homes.
